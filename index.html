About Us

The Chip Station is Duhok's first custom chip kiosk blending international snack fun with Kurdish soul. Whether you bring your favorite chips or pick from ours, we dress them up with bold sauces, local ingredients, and spices inspired by Kurdistan's rich culinary heritage. Fast, fresh, and made to share.
